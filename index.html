Projects

B.Tech Thesis: Frequency Response Analysis of Mosquito Swarming Behaviour

Advisors: Dr. Sachit Butail, Dr. P.B. Sujit
Recorded, processed, and analysed mosquito swarms in a laboratory microcosm. Studied their tracks over different speeds of marker movement for modelling the frequency response. Won second prize in demo presentation at IIITDelhi Research Showcase.

Effect of Leader Placement in Robotic Swarms Control

Qualitative study of the affect of leader placement in robotic swarms. Studied the affect on time to execute a turn, control effort, and the movement of leaders inside the swarm. [Submitted to AAMAS 2017]

Low power device for Snow Petrel’s nest in Antarctica

Device used for detecting temperature in the nests of Snow Petrels (birds found in Antarctica) and their foraging patterns, using Arduino Pro Mini board, temperature and light sensors and a 3D printed enclosure.

Energy Monitoring using PLC Modems

Analysing the repeatability and effectiveness of Powerline Communication Modems in detection low power loads. [Poster abstract was accepted in ACM Buildsys’14 and Microsoft Techvista’15]

Audio localisation using ENF Signatures

Worked on using the ENF signatures embedded within audio recordings for forensic purpose, as part of Signal Processing Cup 2016. [Report]

Low-cost WiFi based temperature sensor

Worked on the current hardware for ESP01 boards at Zenatix, for stabilising and running the WiFi based temperature sensor in AP and Client mode simultaneously.

Ball tracking using quadcopter

Used a Parrot Drone with ardrone package in ROS to track a red ball using OpenCV, Kalman Filtering and PID Control.
